Company History

Afmar Trading and Services was founded by Mazlan Ab Rahim in early 2004. The company is focusing on the operation of making food and drinks in their own kitchens from scratch. This restaurant is located at Senawang, Negeri Sembilan. They created a small restaurant in the residential area and named it as “Warung D’ Tebing”. The restaurant has been operating for 16 years and still maintains their operations until now. Their objective is to serve quality and affordable foods and drinks for the customers. They will make sure all the foods and drinks they are selling are from a good quality of raw materials. It is because they want to ensure customer satisfaction and build a repeat-customer base. Their company has four employees such as a waiter and chef. Their vision is to serve happiness to their customers with delicious, good meals and restaurant experience while working toward the greater good for their employees, community and environment. Their mission is to serve everyone better than anyone else. Warung D’Tebing got a lot of positive feedback in terms of quality and cleanliness. Because of that, their customers increase from time to time. The company’s future plan is to make a system for their small business to ease the operation from Monday to Friday.
